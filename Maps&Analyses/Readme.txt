This folder contains all data necessary to replicate the analyses and maps presented in the paper.

The script “MapsCitiesFinal.R” generates the maps used in Figure 3.
The script “AnalysisCities.R” reproduces all statistical analyses presented in Section "Spatial and temporal exposure of North American urban areas to wildfire smoke" and in "Supplementary Material 1."

The file “MasterSheet.xlsx” contains all publications reviewed for the literature synthesis presented in Section "How do wildfire emissions affect human health?" and "Key research needs and priorities."
